About Me

Welcome to my artistic world! I am Puska, a young artist who is finding her way through various artistic techniques. My work is not limited to one style or medium - I experiment with painting, drawing, and other forms of visual art.


Each piece is an opportunity for me to express emotions, thoughts, and perspectives that surround me. I believe that art has the power to connect people and build bridges between different worlds and experiences.


This portfolio represents my current artistic development and journey to finding my own unique voice in the world of visual arts.
